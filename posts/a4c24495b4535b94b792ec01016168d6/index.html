MSQL开机 High Severity Error

MSQL开机 High Severity Error

错误图片：


错误原因：
mysql的一个配置文件错误，settiongs.config

错误配置：

<?xml version="1.0" encoding="utf-16"?>
<MySQLNotifier>
  <UpdateCheck />
  <WorkbenchMigrationLastAttempt />
  <WorkbenchMigrationSucceeded>True</WorkbenchMigrationSucceeded>
  <AutoAddPattern />
  <MachineList>&lt;?xml version="1.0" encoding="utf-16"?&gt;
&lt;ArrayOfMachine xmlns:xsi="http://www.w3.org/2001/XMLSchema-instance" xmlns:xsd="http://www.w3.org/2001/XMLSchema" /&gt;</MachineList>
  <UseColorfulStatusIcons />
  <CheckForUpdatesFrequency />
  <AutoCheckForUpdates />
  <PingServicesIntervalInSeconds />
  <FirstRun>False</FirstRun>
  <MySQLInstancesList />
  <NotifyOfStatusChange />
  <WorkbenchMigrationRetryDelay>0</WorkbenchMigrationRetryDelay>
  <AutoAddServicesToMonitor />
  <ServiceList />
  <NotifyOfAutoServiceAddition />
  <ServiceSettingsList />
</MySQLNotifier>

正确配置：

<?xml version="1.0" encoding="utf-16"?>
<MySQLForExcel>
<UpdateCheck />
<WorkbenchMigrationSucceeded />
<AutoAddPattern />
<MachineList>&lt;?xml version="1.0" encoding="utf-16"?&gt;
&lt;ArrayOfMachine xmlns:xsi="http://www.w3.org/2001/XMLSchema-instance" xmlns:xsd="http://www.w3.org/2001/XMLSchema"&gt;
  &lt;Machine AutoTestConnectionInterval="10" AutoTestConnectionIntervalUnitOfMeasure="Minutes" MachineId="{153edd09-790a-4a9b-8e77-059b9efeadc2}" Host="localhost" Password="" User="" WMIQueriesTimeoutInSeconds="5"&gt;
    &lt;ServicesList&gt;
      &lt;MySQLService DisplayName="MySQL56" NotifyOnStatusChange="true" ServiceName="MySQL56" UpdateTrayIconOnStatusChange="true" /&gt;
    &lt;/ServicesList&gt;
  &lt;/Machine&gt;
&lt;/ArrayOfMachine&gt;</MachineList>
<UseColorfulStatusIcons />
<CheckForUpdatesFrequency />
<AutoCheckForUpdates />
<FirstRun>False</FirstRun>
<MySQLInstancesList />
<NotifyOfStatusChange />
<WorkbenchMigrationRetryDelay />
<AutoAddServicesToMonitor />
<ServiceList />
<NotifyOfAutoServiceAddition />
<ServiceSettingsList />
</MySQLForExcel>

解决办法

1、打开文件资源管理器：在地址栏输入 *%AppData%\Oracle\MySQL Notifier*

2、删除文件或者按照上面的修改配置文件（删除后重启电脑会自己生成新的文件）
