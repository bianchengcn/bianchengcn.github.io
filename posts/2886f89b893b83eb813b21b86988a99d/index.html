9 个优秀的图片占位图服务

　　在开发网站时，经常需要填充一些图片来预览页面效果，如果设计没有提供的素材，这时我们就需要占位图片，

　　通过 URL 定制形如 placehold.it/300/200 的地址就可以得到一个占位图片。下面就来细数 9 个优秀的占位图片生成网站。

1. https://placeholder.com/

　　BILLIONS & BILLIONS OF PLACEHOLDER IMAGES SERVED

　　特点：老牌服务，支持 HTTPS
　　可定制：尺寸、颜色、文字、格式

  • How To Set Image Size             ------------------>   https://via.placeholder.com/150
  • How To Set Image Formats　　------------------>   https://via.placeholder.com/300.png/09f/fff
  • How To Set Custom Text           ------------------>   https://via.placeholder.com/468x60?text=Visit+Blogging.com+Now

2.http://lorempixel.com/

　　Placeholder Images for every case. Webdesign or Print. It's simple and absolutely free! 

　　特点：图片分类丰富
　　可定制：尺寸、图片分类、文字、灰度图

  • http://lorempixel.com/400/200                 ---------------> to get a random picture of 400 x 200 pixels
  • http://lorempixel.com/g/400/200              --------------->to get a random gray picture of 400 x 200 pixels
  • http://lorempixel.com/1080/1920/sports      ---------------> to get a random picture of the sports category
  • http://lorempixel.com/1080/1920/sports/4/   --------------->to get picture no. 4/10 from the sports category

3.https://fakeimg.pl/

　　　　When designing websites, you may not have the images you need at first.
　　But you already know the sizes and inserting some placeholders can help you better seeing the layout.
　　Don’t waste your time making dummy images for your mockup or wireframe.
　　Fakeimg.pl is a little tool that generates images with an URL.
　　Choose the size, the colors, even the text. It’s free and open-source.

  • 　　<img src="https://fakeimg.pl/250x100/"> <img src="https://fakeimg.pl/250x100/ff0000/">
  • 　　<img src="https://fakeimg.pl/350x200/ff0000,128/000,255">
  • 　　<img src="https://fakeimg.pl/350x200/?text=World&font=lobster">

4.http://placekitten.com/

　　A quick and simple service for getting pictures of kittens for use as placeholders in your designs or code. 

　　Just put your image size (width & height) after our URL and you'll get a placeholder.

　　特点：喵控必备
　　可定制：尺寸、灰度图

　　Like this: http://placekitten.com/200/300
　　or: http://placekitten.com/g/200/300

5.http://fpoimg.com/

  • 　　http://fpoimg.com/300x200
  • 　　http://fpoimg.com/300x200?bg_color=333
  • 　　http://fpoimg.com/300?text=have%20a%20nice%20day!

6.https://picsum.photos/

　　The Lorem Ipsum for photos.

　　Easy to use, stylish placeholders

　　

　　特点：图片来自高质量图库 Unsplash

　　可定制：尺寸、指定图片、模糊图片

  • https://picsum.photos/200/300
  • https://picsum.photos/200

7.https://placeimg.com/

　　特点：支持棕褐色图

　　可定制：尺寸、图片分类、灰度图、棕褐色图

　　<img src="https://placeimg.com/640/480/any">

8.http://temp.im/ (国内）

　　im temp placeholder images, feel free to use it anywhere.

　　特点：国内速度快
　　可定制：尺寸、颜色

9.https://dummyimage.com/

  • https://dummyimage.com/300
  • https://dummyimage.com/300.png/09f/fff
