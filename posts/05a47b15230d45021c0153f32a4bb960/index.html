TokenGazer《一问到底》| 第48期：研究员 VS LINIX

06 19

矩阵链（LINIX）：

是去信任分类账，它跟踪、存储和确认交易和钱包地址，权力制衡证明（PoCB）用以维护平台的共识和安全。在矩阵链中，矿工和验证者共同控制共识。

TokenGazer《一问到底》是一档辨析区块链领域一级市场项目优劣的优质栏目。每一期将针对区块链领域早期的一级市场项目，邀请项目负责人做客现场，和社群内百余名研究员深度问答、科学辨析。旨在通过项目方与研究员高质量的对弈问答，打造专业级别的项目评析平台，厘清项目价值，探寻早期优质项目。同时，让社群用户真正参与价值评析，传递评析方法，在“问与答”中获取价值信息。


矩阵链 CTO    Henry Duong

 TokenGazer 研究员    Tiger



01


TokenGazer Researcher Tiger：

There are many cryptocurrency projects using DAG, such as IOTA, NANO and MIXIN. How is LINIX different from them? Why do you call LINIX “The World's First DAG Infused Blockchain”?


Henry ¦ LINIX ：

LINIX is completely different from existing DAG based networks on the market currently. NANO and IOTA are all trying to use DAG as a replacement data structure for blockchain, LINIX isn’t. We want to use DAG in a way that’s more similar to a Layer 2 scalability and sharding solution, while still being anchored to a blockchain like structure through the use of State Matrices.

In other words, NANO and IOTA are not blockchains, and have issues with centralization and liveness, whereas LINIX is still a blockchain, but we will have a DAG layer on top of the blockchain.

换句话说，NANO、IOTA 不是区块链，并且存在中心化和活跃度的问题，而 LINIX 是带有 DAG 层的区块链。

03


TokenGazer Researcher Tiger：

Usually a DAG transaction needs to be verified by subsequent transactions in a random way, which may result in certain transactions being unverified for a long time. How does LINIX solve this problem?


Henry ¦ LINIX ：

LINIX doesn’t work the same way as existing DAG solutions such as IOTA, which requires centralized authority nodes to function, so it does not have the same problems. Transactions are confirmed nearly instantly upon submission to the DAG without needing to wait for later transactions. This is achieved by leverage the security of the anchoring blockchain, so the DAG component can be tuned for maximal scalability while maintaining decentralization.

04


TokenGazer Researcher Tiger：

According to the LINIX white paper, there are two roles, namely the miner and the witness in the PoCB mechanism. The miner proposes the State Matrix and the witness is in charge of voting verification. What kind of economic incentives does the LINIX system provide for miners? How do you decide which miner has the right to propose the State Matrix?


Henry ¦ LINIX ：

Witnesses are basically just validators in POS consensus. They need to stake a certain amount of tokens to quality, then the selection will be random. Incentives are receiving staking rewards similar to how Ethereum 2.0 will work.

We will have a gas system that powers transactions and provide incentives for nodes to process transactions? No mining node will have any special rights. They can all mine State Matrices based on their view of the network, the one that’s valid and most complete will be accepted by the network through voting.

TokenGazer Community：

"Transactions are confirmed nearly instantly upon submission to the DAG without needing to wait for later transactions." Can you explain this in detail?


Henry ¦ LINIX ：

Because the DAG is sharded in a way so that nodes do not need to have knowledge of the entire transaction graph to process transactions, or check for double spending or conflicting transactions. This allows the network to scale linearly, and transaction processing speed to remains constant.
