10 个优秀的占位图片生成服务

placehold.it

A quick and simple image placeholder service.

特点：老牌服务，支持 HTTPS
可定制：尺寸、颜色、文字、格式

  • http://placehold.it/300x200
  • http://placehold.it/300x200/09f/fff.png
  • http://placehold.it/300&text=world
  • http://placehold.it/300x200/ff9900/fff.png?text=hello

 

lorempixel

Placeholder Images for every case. Webdesign or Print. Just put a custom url in your html and you receive a proper placeholder picture

特点：图片分类丰富
可定制：尺寸、图片分类、文字、灰度图

  • http://lorempixel.com/300/200
  • http://lorempixel.com/300/200/sports
  • http://lorempixel.com/300/200/sports/C7SKY-COM

 

Fake images please?

Fakeimg.pl is a little tool that generates images with an URL. Choose the size, the colors, even the text. Oh… and it’s free.

特点：可指定字体
可定制：尺寸、颜色、文字、字体

  • http://fakeimg.pl/300x200
  • http://fakeimg.pl/300x200/333/EEE/
  • http://fakeimg.pl/300/?text=C7SKY.COM&font=lobster

 

{placekitten}

A quick and simple service for getting pictures of kittens for use as placeholders in your designs or code.

特点：喵控必备
可定制：尺寸、灰度图

  • http://placekitten.com/300/200
  • http://placekitten.com/g/300/200

 

FPOImg.com

provide an easy way to generate placeholder images for your designs whether it be for web development, print, or a school paper

特点：尺寸和文字共同显示
可定制：尺寸、颜色、文字

  • http://fpoimg.com/300x200
  • http://fpoimg.com/300x200?bg_color=333&text_color=EEE
  • http://fpoimg.com/300?text=C7SKY.COM

 

Dynamic Dummy Image Generator

Dynamic Dummy Image Generator

特点：可设定图片比例
可定制：尺寸、等比图、颜色、格式、文字

  • http://dummyimage.com/300x200
  • http://dummyimage.com/300x4:3
  • http://dummyimage.com/300x200/333/EEE.png
  • http://dummyimage.com/300/&text=C7SKY.COM

 

PlaceIMG

Placeholder images for your project in progress. Custom URLs generate placeholder pictures in various sizes with categories and effects.

特点：支持棕褐色图

可定制：尺寸、图片分类、灰度图、棕褐色图

  • http://placeimg.com/300/200
  • http://placeimg.com/300/200/nature
  • http://placeimg.com/300/200/nature/sepia

 

Unsplash it

Beautiful placeholders using images from unsplash

特点：图片来自高质量图库 Unsplash

可定制：尺寸、指定图片、模糊图片

  • https://unsplash.it/300/200
  • https://unsplash.it/300/200/?random
  • https://unsplash.it/300/200?image=7
  • https://unsplash.it/300/200/?blur

 

temp.im（国内）

im temp placeholder images, feel free to use it anywhere. (with https supported)

特点：国内速度快
可定制：尺寸、颜色

  • http://temp.im/300x200
  • http://temp.im/300x200/333/EEE

placeholder.cn（国内）

Quick and simple image placeholders

特点：国内速度快
可定制：尺寸、颜色

  • http://placeholder.cn/300
  • http://placeholder.cn/300x200
  • http://placeholder.cn/300x200/333/EEE
