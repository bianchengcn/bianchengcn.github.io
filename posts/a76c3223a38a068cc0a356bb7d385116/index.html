CSDN Level Help

CSDN blog relies on points to improve its level. Blog points are the recognition and reward of CSDN for the efforts of users, and are also an important standard to measure the level of blogs. Blog rank will also be uniquely determined by blog points.

If you want to quickly level up, you need to follow the CSDN rules to get points, which are as follows:

1. For each original or translated article published: 10 points;

2. For each reproduced article published: 2 points can be obtained;

3. Every time a blogger's article is commented: 1 point can be obtained;

4. Each comment: 1 point can be obtained (no points can be obtained if you give yourself a comment or the blogger replies to a comment);

5, blog reading times more than 100 times: can get 1 point, reading bonus points up to 100 points, that is, the article click tens of thousands of times;

6, the article is voted: top 1 vote plus 1 point, step 1 vote minus 1 point;

7. If the article is deleted by the administrator or the blogger himself, the score obtained by the blogger based on this blog post will be subtracted accordingly;

8. If the comment is deleted by the administrator or the blogger, the score obtained by the commenter and the blogger based on the comment will be subtracted accordingly (the score deducted by the blogger will not be removed dynamically in real time, but will be cleaned once a week);

9, in addition, the corresponding plagiarism report function will be opened. Once the report proves that an original article is plagiarized, the blogger will deduct the corresponding score of the article.
